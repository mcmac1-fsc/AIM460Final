Factual Narrative Triage using Sentence Embeddings

The Challenge: Navigating Legal Ambiguity

Initial charging decisions for legal cases require comparing complex, colloquial narratives against precise legal definitions. Traditional NLP methods often fail due to:

  • Semantic Overlap: Different laws using similar words (e.g., "fear," "harm").
  • Fuzzy Language: Real-world reports use slang, typos, and jargon.
  • Embedding Dilution: Single models struggle to optimize for multiple, distinct legal domains like violence, fraud, and sexual offenses simultaneously.
